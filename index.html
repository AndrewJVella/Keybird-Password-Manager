What is Keybird?

The purpose of Keybird is to improve the accessibility of password managers
without compromising security.
You need lots of passwords but making them is tedious and in-browser managers
can lock you out.

You may ask yourself:
"What was my password again?"
"Why does Safari fill in the wrong info?"
"Did I make this account on Google?"

Skip that nonsense, use Keybird.

Keybird is designed to generate and store passwords on your computer.
You can use Keybird to generate secure passwords from simple keys,
which are easy to create and remember.
